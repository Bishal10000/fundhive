# FundHive - New Features User Guide

## ðŸŽ¯ How to Use the New Features

### 1. **View Your Trust Rating** 
- Go to Dashboard
- See the blue Trust Rating card at the top
- Your score out of 100 with a label (Trusted, Normal, High Risk)
- Based on: Account age, email verification, campaign success, donations made

### 2. **Complete Your Profile Background**
- Click "Edit Profile" button in the Trust Rating card
- Fill in:
  - **Education**: Your educational background
  - **Occupation**: Current job or role
  - **Work History**: Describe your professional experience
  - Background information helps increase your trust rating
- Profile verification badge shows when complete

### 3. **View Your Activity History**
- Scroll down to "Your Campaign Activities" section
- See timeline of all activities:
  - Campaigns created
  - Donations made
  - Campaigns that became successful
  - Comments posted
- Color-coded by activity type with timestamps

### 4. **Track Campaign Creation Timeline**
- Find "Campaign Creation History" section
- Visual timeline of all your campaigns
- For each campaign:
  - Current fundraising status
  - Progress percentage
  - Days remaining
  - Quick links to view or edit

### 5. **Monitor Fraud Detection**
- Dashboard has "Fraud Detection & Monitoring" section
- Shows:
  - Count of flagged campaigns
  - Medium risk campaigns
  - Safe campaigns
- Detailed analysis of each flagged campaign:
  - Fraud risk score
  - Specific risk factors identified
  - Review and action options (for admins)

### 6. **Create Duplicate Campaign Warning**
- When creating a campaign with similar title/content in past 30 days
- Warning appears automatically
- Suggests existing campaign to avoid duplication
- Prevents spam and duplicate campaigns

### 7. **Explore Success Stories**
- Scroll to "Success Stories" section
- View campaigns that successfully reached their goal
- Each story shows:
  - Campaign image
  - Fundraising amount and goal
  - Category
  - Number of donors
  - Success story text (if available)
  - Link to learn more

## ðŸ’¡ FAQ

**Q: Why is my trust rating low?**
A: Complete your profile background, verify email, and ensure campaign descriptions are detailed.

**Q: What if my campaign gets flagged for fraud?**
A: It doesn't mean you're fraudulent. Upload better images, add more detailed description, verify email.

**Q: Can I change my campaign after creation?**
A: Yes, click "Edit" in the Campaign Creation Timeline section.

**Q: How often is fraud detection run?**
A: Continuously as campaigns are created/updated, with batch analysis daily.
